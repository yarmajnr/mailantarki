From Local Heroes to Global Stars: Mailantarki boys across the world!

In just one year of our existence, the ‘Mailantarki Care Football Club’ has achieved remarkable success by transferring 13 young and talented Nigerian players to various European clubs to continue their careers in football.

As the president of the football club and a Gombe State indigent, I made the disclosure during a victory dinner organized by ‘Bilaad Realty’, pioneers of sustainable real estate solutions in Nigeria in Abuja, to celebrate our club’s successes in the 2022 European Tour in both Denmark (Dana Cup) and Norway (Norway Cup) championships.

Our correspondent reports that Mailantarki Care Football Club, in our recent trips to participate in both youth championships in Denmark and Norway, set a new world record of being the first team to win both trophies with unbeaten records, scoring 39 goals in Denmark’s Dana Cup and 35 goals in Norway Cup in 8 matches respectively.

As the governorship candidate of the New Nigeria People’s Party (NNPP) in Gombe State for the 2023 general election, I said my investments to empower youths through football were yielding positive results. I rated our club’s achievements high in areas such as scouting programmes across states in the country, finishing top of NLO group stage and waiting for NNL promotional play-off, successfully transferring no fewer than 13 players to various European clubs, and successful outings in European tours.

As a football enthusiast, I revealed that plans were underway to construct world-class sports facilities to be known as Mailantarki Sports Complex in Abuja.

In his remarks at the event, the Chief Executive Officer of ‘Bilaad Realty’ Aliyu Aliyu said that the reason behind organizing the dinner was to celebrate our glorious team for our feat in winning both trophies in Denmark and Norway.

Aliyu said his company was part of the sponsors for the European trip while restating commitment to continuing in that light towards grassroots football development.
