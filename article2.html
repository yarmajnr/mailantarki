Mailantarki Care Football Academy Wins Norway Cup 2022

The Mailantarki Care Football Academy of Nigeria have emerged winners of the 50th edition of the world's largest Youth Championship, Norway Cup 2022.

The Gombe lads thrashed host KFUM FK 2-0 in the main pitch of the Ekeberg Sports Complex in Oslo on Saturday, August 6, 2022.

Recall that they had on Saturday, July 30, emerged the champions of the 2022 Dana Cup International Youth Championship in Denmark.

The hosts, KFUM, were undone a few minutes before the break after Muhammed Mouro’s beautiful inviting cross from the right flank was brilliantly converted by Miko Dalha in the center, sending Mailantarki’s Norwegian supporters into wild jubilation from the stands.

Abdulaziz Maiyadi, who netted a beautiful shot minutes before the end of the entertaining encounter, emerged as the highest goal scorer for the team with 12 goals, followed by Miko Dalha with eight goals.

The feat achieved by the Khamisu Ahmed Mailantarki boys is the first for a club to go all the way and win both senior categories in Denmark and Norway in the same year.

However, besides the respective glories in both cities of Hjørring and Oslo, the Mailantarki boys’ historic display pulled high attention from top-notch football managers across the two host countries.

Consequently, negotiations have been reached, while others are ongoing, for the Gombe boys to be tied up to professional careers.

The glorious contingent is expected to jet out of Oslo on Monday to Copenhagen en route to Istanbul before connecting to an Abuja-bound plane for the final destination.
